Midnight Yell

Midnight Yell is a tradition at Texas A&M University where people gather to practice the yells that will be performed by the Yell Leaders and A&M fans at the game the following day. Midnight Yell officially became a university tradition in 1931 and has continued every home game since. Students, alumni, or anyone who has the desire to come can attend Midnight Yell to learn more about the rivalry between Texas A&M and the school that is being played the next day, practice the yells that will be used, and get excited for the game.
