Blackout tu is the newest addition to Texas A&M’s traditions. Founded by a group of 4 students in March of 2024, Blackout tu will provide a unifying atmosphere in Kyle Field, Reed Arena, Olsen Field, Davis Diamond, and other sports complexes. At every sporting event against tu, the Aggies will blackout their Longhorn opponents. As the 12th Man and teammates dress in all black, an overwhelming sense of camaraderie will ignite the ecstatic energy the 12th Man is well-known for. This year, Texas’ favorite rivalry is returning to Kyle Field. On November 20, 2024, the Aggies will take on the Longhorns for the first time in 13 years! Blackout tu! BTHO tu!
